Privacy Policy for Alias Wow

This Privacy Policy describes how Kadmiv («we», «our», «us») collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when using our mobile game «Alias WOW» («Game»).

1. Data Collection

We collect the following information when you use the Game:

  • Account Information: We collect data necessary for account creation and management, including your email address.
  • Subscription Information: We collect information related to your subscription, including subscription status and details.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, to optimize the Game’s performance on your device.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Managing and maintaining your account and subscriptions.
  • Processing payments and handling subscription-related tasks.
  • Communicating with you regarding your account and subscriptions.
  • Analyzing game usage to improve the Game and develop new features.
  • Displaying personalized advertisements through third-party services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law. We use third-party services to facilitate our Game and provide services on our behalf, including:

  • Google Analytics: For analyzing game usage and performance metrics.
  • AdMob: For displaying advertisements within the Game.
  • These third-party services may collect and manage data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who need the information to perform their job functions.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Your account and subscription data are retained as long as your account remains active.
  • Inactive Accounts: We may retain your data for up to 12 months after your account becomes inactive before securely deleting it.
  • Data Deletion Requests: Upon receiving a request to delete your personal information, we will promptly remove your data from our systems, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain information.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics: For tracking and analyzing how users interact with the Game. Google Analytics may collect data such as user behavior, session duration, and device information.
  • AdMob: For serving advertisements within the Game. AdMob may collect data related to ad impressions, clicks, and user interactions with ads.
  • These services have their own privacy policies governing their data collection and usage practices.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information that we hold.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • To exercise these rights, please contact us at kadmiv.mail@gmail.com.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or comments about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at kadmiv.mail@gmail.com.
